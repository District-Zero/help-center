# Emotional Intelligence in Medical Education

Emotional Intelligence (EI) plays a crucial role in the success of medical professionals. DistrictZero's Mentorship Program focuses on developing key EI competencies to enhance STEM outcomes.

## Key EI Competencies

1. Self-awareness
2. Self-regulation
3. Motivation
4. Empathy
5. Social skills

## How We Integrate EI

- Regular assessments to track EI growth
- Targeted action plans to develop specific competencies
- Guided check-ins to reflect on EI progress
- Mentor feedback focused on EI development

Learn more about how each user type interacts with EI concepts in their respective documentation sections.
